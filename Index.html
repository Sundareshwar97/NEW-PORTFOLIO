PROJECTS

COIR FIELD ROVER for coir mills. It is Project done for Agriculture Purpose Based On Arduino and Bluetooth Module. In traditional methods, coir industries are using heavy vehicles such as tractor to dry the coir obtained from coconut. These heavy vehicles consume more fuel and it requires skilled personnel to operate the machine. These problems will be overcome by our project, which provides economically viable solution for the problems faced in the coir industries. Small scale industries cannot afford heavy vehicles and employ labors to stir the coir fibers by their hands which may cause health disorders to them. The coir field rover is a small vehicle which is operated by electric motors. These motors are energized by battery. The traction of the rover and rotary rotation are controlled using Arduino - Bluetooth interface. The rotary rotating blades will be rotated at high speed, which will stir the coir fibers.
